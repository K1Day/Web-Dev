Dumplings

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 6-10
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Water 3/4 cup
Ground meat (pork, beef, or chicken) 300 g
Onion 1
Garlic 2 cloves
Soy sauce 2 tbsp
Vegetable oil 1 tsp
Salt and pepper to taste

Step 1: Prepare the dough by mixing the flour and water in a bowl. Knead until smooth, then cover and let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
Step 2: In a separate bowl, combine the ground meat, onion, garlic, soy sauce, vegetable oil, salt, and pepper. Mix until evenly combined.
Step 3: Roll out the dough and cut it into small circles (about 3 inches in diameter).
Step 4: Place a teaspoon of the filling in the center of each circle. Fold the dough over and seal the edges tightly, forming a crescent shape.
Step 5: Boil water in a large pot. Cook the dumplings in batches for about 5-7 minutes, or until they float to the top.
Step 6: Serve hot with soy sauce or your favorite dipping sauce.

Rolls

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4-6
Ingredients Quantity
Sushi rice 2 cups
Rice vinegar 1/4 cup
Seaweed sheets (nori) 6 sheets
Raw fish or cooked seafood 300 g
Avocado 1
Cucumber 1
Soy sauce to serve

Step 1: Cook the sushi rice according to the package instructions. Mix with rice vinegar and let it cool.
Step 2: Lay a sheet of nori on a bamboo rolling mat. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving a small edge uncovered.
Step 3: Add your choice of fillings (fish, avocado, cucumber) in a line across the rice.
Step 4: Roll the nori tightly using the mat. Seal the edge with a little water.
Step 5: Cut the roll into bite-sized pieces. Serve with soy sauce.

French Fries

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Potatoes 4 large
Vegetable oil for frying
Salt to taste

Step 1: Peel and cut the potatoes into thin sticks.
Step 2: Soak the potatoes in cold water for 15 minutes, then pat them dry with a towel.
Step 3: Heat the oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 180°C (350°F).
Step 4: Fry the potatoes in batches for about 4-5 minutes, or until golden and crispy.
Step 5: Remove and place on paper towels to drain excess oil. Sprinkle with salt and serve hot.
